Biruk - Short Film

A gripping story brought to life by young Ethiopian filmmakers

About the Film

"Biruk" is a short film that explores the emotional journey of a young man caught between tradition and change. Set in Addis Ababa, this story reflects the heart of youth navigating identity, family expectations, and modern challenges.
